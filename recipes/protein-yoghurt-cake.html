Protein Yoghurt Cake

Description

Here is a great option for a low calorie high protein snack. Using simple ingredients you can get a sweet treat when that craving hits you!

ingredients

  • 200g Greek Yoghurt
  • 30g Whey Protein of your favourite flavour
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tbsp Corn Flour

Steps

  1. Use a bowl to mix the Yoghurt with Whey protein
  2. Add the egg whites and keep mixing
  3. Add the Corn Flour and mix a final time
  4. Pour the butter in 2 small ramekins
  5. Bake for around 20mins at 170c
