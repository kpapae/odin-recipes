Tuna Salad

Description

Tired from the full and exhausting day you just had? Look what you can prepare in 10 minutes for a quick, easy and healty dinner!

Ingredients

Units:
  • 1 can 1 can of tuna, drained
  • 50g 3tbspn salad dressing of your choice, usually mayonaise is a good option
  • 15g 1 tbspn sweet pickle relish

Steps

  1. Mash the tuna together with the dressing
  2. Mix in the pickle relish
  3. Enjoy!
